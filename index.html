What the columns mean
Matches (played / total)
Matches played at this venue so far, out of the total scheduled. A match counts as played 2.5 hours after its scheduled kickoff. The tracker updates twice a day, so this is usually accurate within about 9 hours of full time.
GDP Impact
Extra money the World Cup brings to this city from visiting fans — as a % of the city's annual economy. Smaller cities scoring high tells the biggest story. Full method ↗
Economic Buzz
Wikipedia and Wikivoyage views for this city this week, vs the same week in 2019, 2022 and 2023. 3.5× = 3.5 times the usual interest. Combines stadium Wikipedia (50%) and city travel pages (50%).
Points
Cities ranked 1st–10th by Economic Buzz earn 10–1 pts each week. 11th–16th score zero.
Why compare to prior years?
Wikipedia traffic rises every summer anyway. Comparing to 2019, 2022 and 2023 removes that seasonal effect and isolates the genuine World Cup impact. Those years had no major football events in these cities during June–July.
