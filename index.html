Methodology
Search terms
"[City] World Cup" — consistent phrasing across all 16 cities for direct comparability.
Interest index
Google Trends relative score 0–100 within each week window. Normalised so the highest-interest city = 100.
Weekly points
Cities ranked by interest index each week. 1st = 10pts, 2nd = 9pts, 3rd = 8pts ... top 10 score, bottom 6 score 0pts. Index is the input; points are the output.
To Date
Sum of points from all completed discrete weeks. Reflects cumulative interest momentum across the tournament.
Week windows
W1: Jun 11–17 · W2: Jun 18–24 · W3: Jun 25–Jul 1 · W4: Jul 2–8. Last Week = rolling 7-day window.
Data refresh
Fetched automatically every Saturday at 08:00 UTC via GitHub Actions. Click Wiki ↗ to open the stadium's Wikipedia article.
